A pill-shaped call-to-action **link** (real `<a>`) for marketing surfaces. Use `Button` instead for in-app form actions.

```jsx
<Cta href="/contact?intent=teaming">Partner with us</Cta>
<Cta href="/capabilities" variant="secondary">View capability statement</Cta>
```

Variants: `primary` (gradient navy), `secondary` (frosted glass). `block` stretches full width.
